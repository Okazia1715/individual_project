# Actionable Research Question

In the context of the stated constraints, the underlying biological  
problem was reformulated into a computationally tractable  
research question.

---

## Main Research Question

Can a machine learning-based segmentation pipeline be developed  
to reproducibly quantify the localization of particles in  
gastrointestinal tissue cells?

---

## Specific Research Questions

1. Can the Cellpose model be trained to reproducibly segment  
   adipocytes from colon tissue images?

2. Can particle localization be classified as inside or outside  
   segmented cells?

3. Can image-level quantitative metrics be extracted  
   reproducibly?

---

## Stated Constraints

- Limited availability of annotated image masks
- Lack of a ground-truth nanoparticle image set
- Project time constraints
- Requirement to ensure reproducibility
